# Module 11: SunCoast Retail Visual Analysis
This repository features a comprehensive data visualization suite built for "SunCoast Retail." Using Python and Matplotlib, this project analyzes quarterly sales trends, advertising efficiency, and customer demographics across multiple Florida markets.

## Files Included
- `module11-assignment.py`: The primary script containing data generation, visualization functions, and a centralized business dashboard

## What I Practiced
In this project, I demonstrated the ability to communicate complex data through various visualization techniques:
- **Time-Series Analysis**: Created line charts to track quarterly sales growth and compared performance trends across four different cities
- **Categorical & Compositional Data**: Developed grouped and stacked bar charts to visualize how different product categories (e.g., Electronics, Clothing) contribute to total revenue by location
- **Relationship & Efficiency Modeling**: Used scatter plots and efficiency line charts to evaluate the correlation between advertising spend and sales revenue
- **Demographic Distribution**: Engineered subplots with histograms and box plots to analyze customer age distributions and purchase behaviors across specific age groups
- **Dashboard Integration**: Built a high-level executive dashboard combining multiple charts to provide a "single pane of glass" view of company growth and market share

## Key Business Insights
- Identified **Miami** as the leading market and **Electronics** as the dominant product category
- Confirmed a significant **Q4 holiday boost** across all locations
- Analyzed the diminishing returns of advertising spend to optimize future marketing budgets
